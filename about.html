  • About Livvy H!

    A creative mind focused on making your branding look right and feel right.

    More Information

    Hi, I'm Livvy! I consider myself a time traveller from outer space (rising designer). I have an affinity for subcultural branding that presents authenticity without the homogenizing touch of corporatism. In my little toolbox (brain), I carry a background in fine arts that lays the foundation for my work across many fields. I have a soft spot for packaging design, particularly cute products with a dark twist. I love traditional tattoo aesthetics, which hope to combine with modern products. In my free time you can find me rummaging through thrift shops and exploring the best cafes in town.
